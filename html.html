About Fitness
Physical fitness is a state of health and well-being and, more specifically, the ability to perform aspects of sports, occupations and daily activities. Physical fitness is generally achieved through proper nutrition, moderate-vigorous physical exercise, and sufficient rest along with a formal recovery plan.

  • Experts define physical fitness as “one's ability to execute daily activities with optimal performance, endurance, and strength with the management of disease, fatigue, and stress and reduced sedentary behavior.”

  • Exercise makes you feel more energized because it releases endorphins into the blood. Movement in exercise helps relieve stress by producing a relaxation response which serves as a position distraction.

  • Social Fitness is the ability to engage in productive personal and professional relationships, positively interact with unit and command networks, and use resources that promote overall well-being. Social fitness is like any other area – the more you practice or exercise it, the stronger your relationships will be.
